CRO tactics aren't magic bullets

Most the popular CRO sites use tactics from Amazon and other large ecommerce companies. They make it sound like a few little changes will reap huge rewards. But, your store has different needs than large ecommerce companies and will gather more info if you are talking with your customers too.

The Numbers Don't Answer "Why?"

Interpeting the numbers often end up being a guess based on what your analytics are telling you, instead of actionable information. Amazon can make a change, get actionable data within hours and know very quickly if it is making a difference or not. It is often a struggle to get enough visitors to landing pages to have enough significance, much less a specific product page, cart, or checkout page.
